BOTOX®– Botox injections are used to soften moderate to severe facial wrinkles. Botox targets the repeated muscle contractions from frowning and squinting over the years. BOTOX® Cosmetic is a prescription medicine that is injected into muscles and used to temporarily improve the look of moderate to severe forehead lines, crow’s feet lines, and frown lines between the eyebrows in adults.
